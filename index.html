Company Overview

Selling since 2021. Still selling today.

We have been doing ecommerce since 2021, starting with a single Amazon account and growing into a multi-marketplace operation run with discipline, data and our own software.

Today we operate across Amazon (UK, USA & Australia) in wholesale brand reselling and private label, eBay dropshipping in USA, UK and Australia, OnBuy UK, and TikTok Shop (UK & USA) on a white-label model.

Alongside our own stores, we provide the same services to clients worldwide — audits, product research, listing SEO, PPC management, store setup and full account management — plus freelancing support for sellers who need an experienced operator on demand.

What makes us different: we build our own automation tools (including a public Chrome extension for OnBuy sellers), we test everything on our own money first, and we give honest feasibility assessments — even when the honest answer is "don't do this".
